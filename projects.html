Projects

This page gives you insight in the various projects I am involved in

Doctoral dissertation

In my doctoral dissertation I focus on Political Micro-targeting on social networking sites. More specifically, the emphasis lies on using disclosures as a measure of transparency to inform users about content not only being sponsored and paid for by a party or organisation but also being targeted to them based on various types of data.

Influencers on Instagram

I am involved in a project with both direct and indirect colleagues from different chairs in which we investigate the credibility of influencers on Instagram for a non-existent fruit and vegetable delivery service.

Metaverse

From both a personal and professional curiosity, I plan on conducting a study on advertising in the Metaverse. To some this might sound as a futuristic 'thing' that will never happen but please don't forget that Blockbuster laughed at Netflix in the nineties.
